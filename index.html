I'm an engineer and researcher working in natural language processing, currently I'm a senior machine learning engineer at Factmata, working on online content understanding, combating misinformation and harmful content.

I have more than four years of experience working in the industry, before Factmata I worked at Mawdoo3 (World's most visited Arabic website), Yaoota (MENA's premier shopping search engine), and Microsoft Research Lab in Cairo.

I collaborate with NLP North research unit at IT University of Copenhagen, under the supervision of Barbara Plank, and published a couple of papers at EACL & NAACL 2021.
